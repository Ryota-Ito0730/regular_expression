▼正規表現

https://calmyou.com/themes/flower
https://calmyou.com/themes/cat
https://calmyou.com/themes/koyo
htt://calmyou.com/themes/dog
http://calmyou.com/themes/summer
http://calmyou.com/themes/spring
https://calmyou.com/information
https://calmyou.com/
https://calmyou.com
calmyou.com/themes/summer/
calmyou.com/themes/spring
calmyou.com/information

例えば、上記のようなURL一覧から正規表現を使用して様々なパターンに応じた行を検索する

□パターン1
「https」ではじまるもののみ指定する場合
[指定方法]
^https

[検索結果]
https://calmyou.com/themes/flower
https://calmyou.com/themes/cat
https://calmyou.com/themes/koyo
https://calmyou.com/information
https://calmyou.com/
https://calmyou.com

□パターン2
「https」と「http」ではじまるのを指定する場合
[指定方法]
^https?

[検索結果]
https://calmyou.com/themes/flower
https://calmyou.com/themes/cat
https://calmyou.com/themes/koyo
http://calmyou.com/themes/summer
http://calmyou.com/themes/spring
https://calmyou.com/information
https://calmyou.com/
https://calmyou.com


□パターン3
□行の途中にcalmyou.comがあるもののみヒットさせる場合
[指定方法]
.calmyou.

[検索結果]
https://calmyou.com/themes/flower
https://calmyou.com/themes/cat
https://calmyou.com/themes/koyo
htt://calmyou.com/themes/dog
http://calmyou.com/themes/summer
http://calmyou.com/themes/spring
https://calmyou.com/information
https://calmyou.com/

※.は任意の1文字を指定する記号であるため、ヒット対象とするURL末尾に/がない場合、
例えば「https://calmyou.com」ではヒットしない


□パターン4
□末尾に「calmyou.com/」があるもののみヒットさせる場合
[指定方法]
calmyou.com/$

[検索結果]
https://calmyou.com/

□パターン5
□末尾に「calmyou.com/」か「calmyou.com」があるもののみヒットさせる場合
[指定方法]
calmyou.com/?$

[検索結果]
https://calmyou.com/
https://calmyou.com
